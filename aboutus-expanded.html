WHO CAN APPLY?

Following food businesses (registration, state license & central license category) can apply for Hygiene Rating Scheme:

  • Food service establishments (such as hotels, restaurants, cafeterias, dhabas, etc), sweet shops, bakeries, and meat retail stores
  • Meat Retail Shop
  • Sweet Shop
  • Bakery Shops

HOW TO GET HYGIENE RATING CERTIFICATE FOR YOUR ORGANIZATION?

The Food Business Operator applying for Hygiene Rating must comply with the following requirement:

  • Have an active FSSAI License/ Registration, follow schedule 4 requirements and display FSDBs.
  • Appoint FoSTaC trained Food Safety Supervisors
  • Get food and water samples tested periodically (applicable for licensed FBOs only)
  • Apply to us for Hygiene Rating Audit & get your certificate.

PROCESS OF OBTAINING HYGIENE RATING CERTIFICATE WITH US :

  • Connect with us through email/call and schedule an audit to verify compliance wrt Hygiene Rating Scheme.
  • We will assign the sector-specific auditor and share the audit plan with you for approval.
  • Auditor will visit your food premises and conduct an audit to assess compliance with Hygiene Rating Scheme requirements.
  • The auditor will submit the audit report to us (Hygiene Rating Audit Agency)
  • We will then approve the report.
  • Once the report is approved, we will download the certificate and send it to you
  • You’ll then display the Hygiene Rating Certificate at a prominent location on your premise

HOW CAN YOU IMPROVE YOUR HYGIENE RATING?

Any FBO desirous of improving its ratings can do so by taking the following steps:

  • Check the practices not followed as per the above audit checklist and arrive at the issues. Refer to the Schedule 4 of 4 of the FSS (Licensing & Registration of Food Businesses) Regulation, 2018.
  • Take clear, concrete steps to ensure and demonstrate compliance for each of the non-compliance.
  • Redo self-assessment and connect with us for audit.
  • Print the new verified hygiene rating certificate online.
